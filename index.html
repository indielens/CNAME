GThomas Design

Grant Thomas is a user experience designer based in Indianapolis, Indiana.

His work combines a background at a leading Indianapolis design and strategy firm with technology and user-centered design thinking. Grant currently works at DMI, where he is responsible for all phases of the UX process, from researching to wireframing, prototyping and collaborating with development teams to create an end product that balances business and user needs. Grant advises visual designers and developers throughout the lifecycle of a project to help them meet high standards for usability and design. Throughout his career, Grant has created brands, products, and experiences for a wide range of brands in SaaS, healthcare, funeral service, food delivery, and nonprofit.

Grant received his BFA in Communication Design from the Covenant College in 2014. His foundation in design thinking informs his work as a UX designer. He has guest lectured at Kansas State University. In 2015, Grant won a 5x5 grant to publish an interactive coloring book on Indianapolis. He has served on the board for Indianapolis’ Independent Music & Arts Festival since 2015. He has a passion for accessibility, equality, and diversity.
